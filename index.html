What to evaluate in this prototype

Success criteria from issue #82: a user should be able to (1) find a cashmere product, (2) understand its composition and qualities, (3) add it to cart and reach checkout — without guidance.

1
Can you identify the composition of the Navy Crewneck without clicking anything extra?
2
Is the fit label system (Classic / Relaxed / Slim) clear enough to replace a traditional size chart?
3
Does the return policy feel prominent enough in the PDP and cart to reduce purchase anxiety?
4
Does the cart drawer feel smooth, or does it interrupt the browsing flow?
5
Do the shop filters make sense? Are any missing or confusing?
6
Is one-page checkout (all sections visible at once) better than multi-step? Any friction?
7
On mobile: is the sticky ATC bar helpful or intrusive? Does the hamburger menu feel right?
8
Does the CHF pricing and Swiss identity (Swiss Post, Zurich address) feel trustworthy?
UX Recommendations implemented

Based on competitive audit D8 — critical (R1–R5) and high priority (R6–R10).

R1 · Critical

Material Specs Block

Composition, fibre fineness (micron), weight (gsm) and origin on every PDP. No competitor does this in-page. Visible on the PDP before any click.

R2 · Critical

Fit Labels

Per-size fit label below size selector: "Classic Fit — true to standard sizing". Replaces Lunu's "Free Size" (conversion killer) with actionable guidance.

R3 · Critical

Return Policy on PDP

Trust bar below ATC + one-line reassurance. Repeated in cart drawer footer. None of 3 competitors show this on the product page.

R4 · Critical

Slide-in Cart Drawer

Cart slides from right — no page navigation. Maintains browsing flow for cross-sell. Includes free shipping progress bar and trust line.

R5 · Critical

CHF-Native + Swiss Identity

CHF throughout. Swiss Post delivery. Zurich address in footer. TWINT visible (marked as MVP). GOBI fails this — our structural advantage.

R6 · High

Live Filter System

AJAX-style filtering (no reload) across 6 dimensions. Desktop dropdowns + mobile accordion drawer. Active chips + "Clear all". Live results count.

R7 · High

Composition on Product Cards

"100% 2-ply cashmere · 280 gsm" visible in the grid without clicking. Neither Lunu nor GOBI shows composition on cards — highest-value single improvement.

R8 · High

Sticky ATC Bar (Mobile)

Fixed bar at bottom viewport appears when ATC scrolls out of view on mobile PDP. Shows product name + selected size. Disappears when ATC is visible.

R9 · High

Curated Cross-sell

"Also pairs well with" — 3 hand-selected products per PDP (same colour family/styling context). Manual curation beats algorithm for 12-product catalog.

R10 · High

One-Page Checkout

Contact / Delivery / Payment visible simultaneously. Express pay (Apple/Google Pay) at top. Persistent order summary right panel. Return policy below Pay button.
